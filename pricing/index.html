Pricing

Choose QuantumCache Free for public WordPress acceleration, or QuantumCache Pro for WooCommerce-aware behavior, controlled logged-in caching, metrics, CDN-aware integration, and broader operational control.

Free

QuantumCache Free

Free is the baseline tier for public WordPress acceleration. It uses the shared QuantumCache engine to reduce repeated database and rendering work for anonymous traffic.

  • Query caching and fragment caching inside PHP
  • Anonymous front-end caching for public traffic
  • Redis as the preferred store, with MySQL fallback
  • Tag-based invalidation and per-site cache namespaces
  • Admin controls for TTLs, debug headers, and purges
  • wp quantumcache status and wp quantumcache flush

Best fit for standard WordPress sites, or WooCommerce deployments where generic public-page caching is enough and no WooCommerce-specific policy is required.

Pro

QuantumCache Pro

Pro includes everything in Free and extends it for more complex WordPress and WooCommerce behavior where correctness, control, and observability matter.

  • WooCommerce-aware cache variation for sorting, filters, locale, currency, and related catalog state
  • WooCommerce-specific bypass rules for cart, checkout, account, AJAX, and session-driven requests
  • Broader invalidation for product, stock, review, order, term, and selected catalog-setting changes
  • Controlled logged-in caching for allowlisted roles with a separate TTL
  • Prometheus-compatible metrics and operator tooling
  • CDN-aware cache-tag headers and purge integrations

Best fit for WooCommerce stores, limited logged-in acceleration, and teams that need stronger operational controls around cache behavior.

Which Tier Fits

Free and Pro share the same core cache model. The decision is mainly about how much policy and operational surface your site needs around that engine.

Free

Free may be enough if you need:

  • Faster public WordPress delivery
  • The shared QuantumCache query and fragment engine
  • Core admin controls, purge tools, and WP-CLI basics
  • A simpler deployment focused on anonymous traffic
Pro

Pro may be worth evaluating if you need:

  • WooCommerce-aware handling for filtered, sorted, or localized catalog traffic
  • Safer cache behavior around cart, checkout, account, and session-driven requests
  • Limited logged-in caching for selected roles
  • Metrics, CDN integration, or broader operational controls
